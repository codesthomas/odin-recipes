Sandwich

Sandwich Recipe from Thomas Lopez

Description:

This Sandwich receipe is quite tasty and enjoyable.

Not expensive, unless you use deli meats.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • Ketchup or Mustard
  • Three slices of Ham
  • 1 slice of Mild Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Take 2 slices of sandwich bread and place on plate next to each other
  2. Place three slices of ham on left slice of sandwich bread
  3. Take your slice of cheese and place on right slice of sandwhich bread
  4. Squeeze small amount of ketchup or mustard on either slice of bread
  5. Close sandwhich slices on top of each other

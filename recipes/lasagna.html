Lasagna

Lasagna Recipe from Thomas Lopez

Description:

This lasagna receipe is quite tasty and enjoyable.

Not expensive, well, other than the ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1.2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1.2 pound lean/fatty ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles/pasta
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce (such as tomato sauce)
  • salt/pepper to taste (2-4 tablespoons)
  • garlic to taste (2 tablespoons)

Steps

  1. Set oven to 350Â°F, allow 5+ min to preheat
  2. boil lasagna noodles
  3. drain while rinsing the noodles
  4. mix beef with garlic and salt/pepper
  5. cook beef with spices/seasoning on medium heat until browned
  6. stir pasta sauce with salt/pepper and garlic to taste for 3-5 minutes
  7. combine mozzarella and parmesan cheese with salt
  8. spread pasta sauce on pan while placing lasagna noodles over the sauce
  9. spread meat over noodles
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes

Hotdog

Hotdog Recipe from Thomas Lopez

Description:

This Hotdog receipe is quite tasty and enjoyable.

Not expensive, at all.

Ingredients

  • Weiner
  • Bread Bun
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Remove bread bun from bag
  2. remove weiner from packaging
  3. place weiner in between bread bun
  4. grab ketchup
  5. lightly apply ketchup to hotdog
